MBA IN DIGITAL MARKETING

Bengal Institute of Business Studies is the only business school with an MBA in Digital Marketing program in West Bengal in collaboration with the Globally recognised institute DMI Ireland. A regular 2 year MBA under Vidyasagar University, a NAAC accredited, W.B. State Government University, recognized by UGC, Ministry of HRD, Govt. of India designed for candidates who want to stay ahead of the curve with global opportunities in hand.

Highlights

MBA in Digital Marketing : Get a Regular Full Time MBA in Digital Marketing from a State Govt. University, the first of its kind in West Bengal. of India.

Exclusive DMI Accreditation: BIBS is the only DMI Accredited Institution in West Bengal to deliver the DMI PRO certification. The program has been designed and curated by the Top 1% Globally recognised marketers who works as the Advisory Council for DMI keeping in view the current industry trends. 20 lakh jobs will be available by 2020.

Harvard Business School Online: Collaboration with HBS online for a certification in Business Analytics & Data science, providing expert training from HBS online. You also get access to the HBS Online Alumni Network.

Industry Lead Training: All PGPDM students become eligible for a 6 to 12 months of the paid Industry Lead Training Program where they work with various client side or agency side companies for experiential learning which helps them in getting accustomed to the industry before their final placements.

The DMI Advantage

DMI PRO The DMI PRO certification is considered as the Global Standard for Digital Marketing education. The course structure is constantly updated keeping in mind the latest industry trends and practices. The certification equips the students with the know-how along with the insights and experience to work and speak with authority in a fast paced and dynamic industry.

Internationally Curated Program The DMI PRO curriculum is developed in collaboration with the Global Industry Advisory Council (GIAC) which consists of the top 1% Globally Recognised marketers from companies including Google, Meta, Microsoft, LinkedIN, Twitter, Coca Cola and more. The GIAC involvement makes sure that the program is curated and developed keeping in mind the current industry trends and standard.

International Internship Opportunities Students who complete the DMI PRO certification will receive the opportunity in applying for an Internship in Australia through a competitive application process. The internship period will range for a period of 8 to 11 months with a stipend of 1,75,000 INR/month.

Globally Recognised CertificationThe DMI Program is considered to be the only Globally Recognised and industry accredited and validated digital marketing curriculum that is delivered in over 110 countries across the world. It also carries a globally transferable qualification which is recognised from industry to industry as well as country to country which ensures greater career mobility.

Webinars from International Thought Leaders The DMI PRO program provides the students with an extensive Knowledge Portal with resources of unparalleled value. Students get access to live, interactive webinars every month from industry leaders around the world on topics which ensures the delivery of industry insights in real time. The access to the DMI community ensures a stronger networking approach and opens up opportunities.

Skillsets Development The program is designed in a method that cuts out outdated practices and blends in the approach of both theoretical and practical knowledge that helps students develop the required skill sets which are practiced and valued globally across industries. The overall professional development of students allows them to retain the certification and utilize it to open up international opportunities.

MBA in Digital Marketing

The MBA - PGPDM and DMI PRO makes the Digital Marketing curriculum a globally recognised program which imparts all the skill sets required to grow and sustain one’s career in a dynamic industry and stay updated with the latest industry practices that keeps the students at par with the corporate requirements.

Our faculty comprises digital marketing professionals from the best agencies and corporates in India as well as experienced academicians who have been associated with the Digital industry for years, who bring a wealth of experience into the classroom.

Every quadmester includes live projects where students get the opportunity to apply their knowledge and create real-life digital marketing campaigns that contribute to the marketing strategies of some of the top corporations in India.

The Program incorporates the disciplines of Business Analytics & Business Intelligence that helps the students to learn these core disciplines which opens up the field of Data Analytics and apply for companies hiring in these roles.

Students get access to industry lead training after 12 months of classroom training which sharpens the acumen of the students and gives them valuable exposure to the day-to-day activities in either a Digital Marketing Agency or the Digital Marketing Department of leading companies in the country.

Campus placement opportunities are provided after the end of internship, with a pool of over 200 reputed national and international companies. BIBS ensures training for placements, covering aptitude tests, group discussions, personal interview preparation and psychometric tests to help the students crack placement processes with ease.

The Entrepreneurship Project in the MBA-PGPDM curriculum makes sure that students get the opportunity to take part in the development of a company and render its services to generate real time business through the BIBS Incubation Center.

The MBA-DM syllabus is constantly updated to keep pace with the dynamic changes happening in the digital marketing arena. With inputs from both the industry and academia, the BIBS course material is one of the most advanced in the industry, thereby ensuring that our students can always utilize their learning in live projects.

BIBS’ proven and time-tested Mentoring Program assures mentoring by experienced faculty. From creating digital marketing campaigns, honing their soft skills, events and competitions to industry visits, mentors are with students all the way till their final placements. BIBS has a tie - up with Toastmasters International, a USA based company, for mentoring on Communication.

Digital Marketing with Business Analytics & Business Intelligence

The industry curated curriculum is aimed at providing a detailed insight of the key concepts, tools and techniques used in the world of Business and Data. This comprehensive training is developed jointly by BIBS and IBM which opens up a track road of exploring the Business Analytics & Intelligence field which are considered to be the must have skill sets in today’s corporate world. The headhunt for Digital Marketers with Analytics skills has increased due to the changing service propositions and client requirements.
